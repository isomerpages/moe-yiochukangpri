<p>YCKPS has embarked on a twinning partnership with Nanhai Foreign Language
School (NFLS) in Guangdong (Foshan), China in 2025. This twinning experience
serves to provide students with extensive exposure to the learning of Chinese
Language and Chinese Culture in an enriched environment; enable students
to develop global awareness and cross-cultural skills; and provide opportunities
for students to learn and interact with peers of another nationality through
the school’s twinning experience at YCKPS’ twinning school in Guangdong
(Foshan), China.</p>

<p>During the learning trip, a group of Primary 5 students visited YCKPS’
twinning school, Nanhai Foreign Language School (NFLS), and were immersed
in the learning of Mathematics which was integrated with calligraphy; Science
through robotics; and culture through traditional craft-making. These lessons
were taught in Chinese Language and the students attended these lessons
with their peers from NFLS. Additionally, one of our Science Teacher (Mr
Louis Loo, Year Head/Middle Primary) also conducted an English-medium Science
lesson in NFLS where YCKPS students were engaged in learning with their
peers from NFLS.</p>

<p>Besides the learning in NFLS, the students also learnt through new and
authentic experiences at the Guangdong Science Centre (which is the largest
in the world), Guangdong Museum, Guangzhou Urban Planning and Exhibition
Centre and the Guangzhou Library. It was an immersive, authentic and rich
learning experience for the students.</p>
